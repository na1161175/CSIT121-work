Mandala Png Illustration Graphics

This image shows a Mandala and has PNG file type. PNG stands for Portable Network Graphic and it supports millions of colors, multiple levels of transparency and interlacing.It uses lossless compression and is well-supported by modern browsers. Growing up, my sister used to make these and ask us to color these just to keep us busy. This brought back a childhood memory, thats why I used this image.
