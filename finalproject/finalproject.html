Introduction

Hello and welcome! I'm Khadija Nadeem, and this is my CSIT-121 web development portfolio. Here, I showcase the labs and projects that reflect my progress in HTML, CSS, responsive design, accessibility. This website binds all my coursework from the semester into one cohesive portfolio.

Navigate through the menu to explore my work and see how my understanding of web development has evolved.
